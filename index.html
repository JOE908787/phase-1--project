Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I book a ride? Simply enter the departure and destination locations and click "Search Routes".
  • Can I change my route? Routes cannot be changed once booked, but you can cancel and rebook.
  • How can I rate my driver? After your ride, you will have an option to rate your driver on a scale from 1 to 5.
